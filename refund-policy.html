My Public Square Returns Policy

Last updated: 25 October 2023.

Home >> Refund Policy

We understand that there may come a time to return a purchase and we aim to make the returns process as simple as possible.

If youre looking to return or exchange your order, we offer free returns within 14 days of purchase. You can return your product for a refund to the original payment method.

Please note: Any request to cancel services will be considered only after a thorough review and prior to the commencement of the service. Should any work, including man hours, have been initiated, the corresponding cost will be deducted before processing the refund.

Return Process

To return an item, email us at info@myps.in with your Order Number and return reason andour support team will be happy to assist you with the next steps.

Return shipping charges will be paid for by us.

Questions


For questions relating to our returns policy, please contact us at info@myps.in
