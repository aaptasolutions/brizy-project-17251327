FOR Celebrities

360-Degree Digital Marketing & PR Services for Celebrities

why My public square

In an age where fame is as fleeting as it is rewarding, My Public Square is your steadfast partner, ensuring that your brilliance never dims. By blending expertise with genuine warmth, we offer solutions that don't just react well to the world of celebrity but proactively shape it.

Smart Solutions for the Modern Celebrity

In today's rapidly evolving digital landscape, celebrities face an ever-expanding set of challenges and opportunities. At My Public Square, we recognize & appreciate the nuanced demands of fame in the 21st century.

We offer a comprehensive suite of services designed to Amplify, Protect, and Refine your digital presence. These services are tailored to match the modern celebrity's needs and requirements. 

The most essential celebrity handling and management services are the following.

They have been crafted to ensure your star continues to shine bright and strong:


Personal Branding

We have keenly witnessed how the very idea of celebrity has changed in the last few decades. Its no longer confined to where you belong- screen, stage, social circuits, or other professional spheres. These niches often overlap, making it more confusing.


The explosive growth of the Internet, coupled with increased personalization, demands you to be one who has a limelight-grabbing personality with a sizable & dedicated fanbase. 


You will be expected to have an opinion on topics and events far removed from your profession, with the power to influence people through your actions or messages, all while retaining a deep connection with the general population.

Now that superstardom is becoming rarer and media demarcations are blurring fast, your celeb-quotient is facing increasingly fierce competition.

My Public Square helps you deal with these challenges in personal branding. 


As of August 2023, there are 4.9 billion active social media users. On these platforms, you are a celebrity and a major brand. We collaborate closely with you, capture your unique essence, and transform it into a more personal narrative. This approach to building a brand ensures a more uniform and authentic representation across all digital platforms.

Strategic Social Media Management

The power of social media - as we touched upon - is too big and influential to fully contain and control. Let our SMM teams be your strategic partners, curating and crafting content that strikes a chord with your audience and deepening the bond you share with them. 

With meticulous planning, we manage posting schedules, engagement strategies, and fan interactions, maintaining a consistent and absolutely genuine voice for you.

Online Reputation and Presence Management

Your digital legacy should stand tall, untainted by the ebb and flow of the Internet and tabloid tides. Our dedicated team, equipped with cutting-edge tools, tirelessly monitors your online image, often in real-time. We combat misinformation, celebrate your milestones, and make sure the digital universe always sees your best side.

Media Relations & PR

Celebrity narratives are often shaped by the media. This includes both the mainstream and exclusive or niche news (and rumour) outlets operating online.


Our seasoned media relations team liaises with influential journalists, bloggers, and trend-spotters to ensure your story shines through- both accurately and positively. Be it press releases, exclusive features, or event appearances, we create, craft, and maintain a consistent and positive narrative with no obvious spin.


Because too much spin tends to backfire.

Paid Ad Campaigns

To ensure you remain in the spotlight, our specialists design robust paid advertising campaigns across platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and Google. 

We go one step ahead and run cutting-edge campaigns on such platforms as X (formerly Twitter), LinkedIn, Pinterest, TikTok, WhatsApp, and Snapchat.


Tailored to showcase your ventures, initiatives, values, works, and ethos, these campaigns ensure that your presence is felt far and wide, attracting both dedicated fans and new admirers in equal measure.

Engaging Content Creation

Move beyond ordinary Facebook posts and tweets; trust us to deliver rich content which captures hearts. Our team of talented creators conjure bespoke videos, articles, and dynamic imagery that articulate your story compellingly. Whether it's a behind-the-scenes look, a charity you're passionate about, or personal anecdotes, we turn them into captivating digital tales.


And people remember well-told stories.

Comprehensive Analytics & Reporting

We excel in these seemingly boring, backroom activities. Our mantra is simple: decisions grounded in data are decisions bound for success. With our comprehensive analytics and reporting, we translate the vast world of metrics into actionable insights. Our transparent reviews and pointers ensure youre always abreast of campaign performances, aiding in future strategy formulations that will increase your celeb quotient.

Join us on this voyage. Together, we can chart a course through the stars, taking your fame above and beyond what you ever expected.
